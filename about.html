About us

I've decided to build this platform with this idea: No learner in Sikkim should ever have to pay for access to knowledge.

Let me share something deeply personal with you. I've spent much of my life as an advocate for free information because I know firsthand the transformative power it holds. Growing up in a place where opportunities can feel as distant as the Himalayan peaks, I turned to the internet as my greatest teacher. Not once have I spent a single dime on learning through it - and neither should you.

Every skill I've honed, every insight I've gained, came from the vast, open sea of knowledge online. It breaks my heart to think of the countless dreams stifled by paywalls, of young minds in Sikkim yearning to grow but held back by the notion that wisdom must come at a price. We no longer live in that archaic age; the internet and AI have shattered those chains, making education a birthright, not a commodity. In a world where AI democratizes learning, crafting personalized notes and tests at the snap of a finger, there is no use hoarding knowledge behind velvet ropes. So I decided to channel it into this space, where generosity reigns.

Credit where it's due: AI has been my silent partner in this revolution, saving immense costs on tech and opening doors I could never have imagined. It's leveled the playing field, turning what was once elite into everyday magic. And in this new dawn of era, remember this: taste is the new skill. In an ocean of free information, it's your discerning eye—your ability to curate, to choose wisely—that will set you apart. Cultivate it here, with me.

Let's build a future where no one is left behind.
