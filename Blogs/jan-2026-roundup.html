Sikkim Current Affairs: Monthly Roundup (Jan 2026)

Published on Feb 15, 2026

Staying updated with state-specific current affairs is crucial for any SPSC aspirant. January 2026 was a dynamic month for Sikkim, marked by significant policy announcements, environmental initiatives, and state awards. Here's a comprehensive roundup of what you need to know.

1. Green State Policy Enhancements

Continuing its legacy as an environmental trailblazer, the Government of Sikkim announced the second phase of the "Sikkim Green Horizons" initiative. This phase heavily regulates single-use plastics in the northern regions and introduces state subsidies for eco-tourism ventures led by local youth.

2. Teesta River Revitalization Project

Following last year's comprehensive study, a multi-crore project has been sanctioned by the central and state governments collaboratively to ensure the ecological health and sustained flow of the Teesta River. The board overseeing this includes environmental experts from Sikkim University.

3. Sikkim State Meritorious Awards

In the field of arts and culture, several distinguished personalities were honored on Republic Day. This year's top honors went to prominent local authors and social workers whose efforts have significantly elevated the preservation of indigenous Sikkimese languages.

4. Economic Developments and Tourism

The state has seen a 15% increase in winter tourism compared to the previous year. To manage this sustainably, a new "Conscious Tourism" app was launched, guiding visitors on minimizing their ecological footprint while exploring the state's natural beauty.

5. Sports and Infrastructure

A new state-of-the-art sports complex was inaugurated in Gangtok, aimed at providing international-level training facilities for athletes, particularly focusing on archery and boxing—sports where Sikkimese athletes have historically excelled.

Questions related to these regional topics frequently appear in both prelims and mains. Ensure you integrate these facts into your broader preparation strategy. Stay tuned for next month's update!
